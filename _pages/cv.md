Research Profile
======

My research focuses on stellar spectroscopy, stellar populations, Galactic archaeology, and the connection between individual-star studies of the Milky Way and stellar population studies of external galaxies. I have worked extensively with MUSE integral-field spectroscopy, machine-learning methods for stellar parameter and abundance inference, and forward modelling of stellar population observations.

I have published 16 research papers, including five first-author papers, and have contributed to major collaborations including GALAH, GECKOS, and oMEGACat.
